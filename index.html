The Craft

Not Factory Made.
Made With Love At Home.

Salaams! We are a proud Muslim family based right here in Stanger. For us, biltong isn't just food; it's about culture, quality, and trust.

That is why we guarantee that all our beef is sourced exclusively from Halaal-certified butcheries. We prepare it in our home with traditional spices and a lot of love. We don't use machines to slice—every piece is hand-cut to ensure you get the perfect texture every time.

It's fresh, wet, and 100% Halaal. Enjoy it with complete peace of mind.
